At UAB football games, Blaze does push ups after each Blazer score. After the first Blazer touchdown (and point after), Blaze does 7 pushes ups.

After the second touchdown and point after, the score is now 14 and Blaze does 14 push ups.

Write a program that calculates how many total push ups Blaze does during the whole game. Assume that only 7-point touchdowns (including the point after) occur. Prompt for the final score and print out how many push ups Blaze has done.

Example 1:
Enter final score: 21
Push ups: 42

Example 2:
Enter final score: 7
Push ups: 7
